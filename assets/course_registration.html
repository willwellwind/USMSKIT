Course Registration 2013/2014 Semester 1

05/08-08/09
Course registration using e-Daftar via Campus Online.
  • Students with academic status P1/P2 are not allowed to register during this period.
09/09
Course registration with PKDT in respective schools.
  • For all students including:
    • Students with academic status P1/P2
    • Students with academic statis active who failed or having problem registering through e-Daftar.
10/09-18/10
Late course registration with PKDT in respective schools.
  • Including course adding or dropping
  • Students must have strong reasons to register or add a course or will receive a fine of RM50
  • Students are not fined for dropping courses during this phase (except for co-curricular courses and language courses which are allowed to be dropped without fine in the first week only)
